Minions

Making their master happy was the tribe’s very reason for existence.

a group of people of the same race, and with the same customs, language, religion, etc, living in a particular area and often led by a chief.

With the emergence of the Stone Age came the rise of a new species.

start to exist, to appear or become known

But keeping a boss, therein lies the rub.

Suggests that the real challenge or problem is in keeping a boss, therein lies the rub = that is where the problem is = there’s the rub.

The Minions took an instant liking to man.

happening immediately

Ancient Egypt held great promise.

Sth or sb showed a lot of potential or indicated (point out, show, be a sign of something) a high likelihood (the chance of sth happening; how likely sth is to happen) or future success

One particular employer took their failure very very badly.

Someone reacted strongly and negatively to their own failure or mistake, it implies a deep sense of disappointment, regret, or emotional pain in response to not succeeding or meeting one’s own expectations

And then, when all hope seemed lost, they found sanctuary.

A place of refuge (a condition or place of safety, where one is protected from danger, distress (it conveys a sense of extreme sorrow, suffering, or discomfort), or trouble.) or safety, a sanctuary can be a place where people or animals are protected and cannot be harmed or captured.

Years passed as the Minions forged their own civilization.

Implies a sense of active creation or shaping, often through hard work and determination, Minions built, developed, or established their own unique society or culture over time.

If this continued any longer, the Minions would perish.

Perish is a formal or literary word that means to die, especially in a sudden or terrible way, suggests a serious, dire (describes a situation or event that is extrmely serious or urgent, often involving great risk or suffering.) consequence, emphasizing the severity of the situation. The statement conveys a sense of urgency or danger, implying that the continuation of a certain event or circumstance would lead to the demise (refers to a person’s death or the end of sth’s existence or operation.) or extinction of the Minions.

Truth be told, Stuart had no idea what he was chosen for, but was thrilled it made people cheer for him.

Stuart was extremely excited, pleased or happy. It conveys a strong feeling of elation (a feeling of great joy) or joy.

Attend guest lectures from esteemed villains, make contacts in the underworld community.

Esteemed means highly respected or admired.

These adorable little freaks are headed to Orlando, too!

Tranditionally, freak can refer to sth very unusual or unexpected, sometimes carry a negative or derogatory(showing a critical or disrespectful attitude) connotation(additional meaning carries beyond its literal definition).

Dad! We got company! It’s because I tripped the alarm.

Got company is an informal way to say tha someone is approaching or has arrived, typically in an unexpected or unwelcome manner.

What a small world! Hope we’re not in rival gangs.

Potential conflicts or differences between the two people meeting.

Oh, Kevin, you don’t wanna work for him! He ate his last henchmen!

Henchmen refers to loyal and subservient followers or helpers, expecially those who engage in criminal activities for a powerful leader or boss.

Ten times the evil in half the package.

This phrase describe sb/sth is extremely malevolent(having or showing a desire to harm other people) or harmful while being deceptively(likely to make you believe sth that is not true) small or unassuming in appearance.

You see this tiny little trinket?

Trinket refers to a small ornament(an object that is used as decoration in a room, garden/yard, etc. rather than for a particular purpose) or item of jewelry that is of little value.

Love the constume!

The clothes worn by actors in a play or movie, or worn by sb to make them look like sth else; The clothes worn by people from a particular place or during a particular historical period.

Buckle up, boys!

Literally, it’s directive to fasten their seatbelts for safety. Figuratively, it’s playful way to say “Get ready for an adventure!”.

But right now, it’s leaking radiation like you would not believe!

It means that radiation is being emitted or released from a particular source.
Like you would not believe is an idiomatic expression used to emphasize the extent or degree of sth.

Herb, these are the new recruits.

It refers to individuals who have recently joined a group, organization, team, or cause.

Right on! You guys are crazy little and way yellow, and I dig that!

Right on is an expression of strong support, approval, or encouragement.
Dig is another common slang term for like. For instance, I dug that movie.

Oh, I love England. The music, the fashion. I’m seriously thinking about overthrowing it someday.

Overthrowing refers to forcibly removing or displacing a system, government, ruler, or authority from power.

You can use it to hypnotize anyone. Anyone!

Hypnotize refers to the ability to put someone into a trance-like(a state in which sb seems to be asleep but is aware of what is said to them) state, making them susceptible(very likely to be influenced, harmed or affected by sb/sth) to suggestions or controlling their action, typically using a specific tool or method.

I feel like a proud mama with three dashing evil sons.

Attractive, confident, fashionable and elegant.

All the three little piggies had to do was just steal on little crown that the beautiful wold had wanted ever since she was a penniless little street cub, unloved and abandoned.

Being penniless means she was without money, suggesting poverty or hardship. The term “street cub“ implies she was young and alone, roaming the streets, likely fending for herself.

So the wolf huffed and puffed, and she blew them off the face of the earth!

The wolf’s actions are described in a much more intense manner, suggesting that she completely obliterated(eliminate) or got rid of the pigs in a significant way.

You’re not allowed in without an adult. Scram, hooligans!

Scram is a colloquial(used in conversation but not in formal speech or writing) term that means “go away” or “leave immediately”.
Hooligans refers to people, typically young, who are noisy, rowdy(making a lot of noise or likely to cause trouble), or disruptive.

This is a restricted area!

Restricted means access to this particular location or space is limited or controlled.

You think it’s funny to mock the elderly, do you?

To mock sb means to ridicule(unkind comments that make fun of sb/sth or make them look silly), mimic(to copy the way sb speaks, moves, behaves, etc., especially in order to make other people laugh), or make fun of them, often in a mean-spirited or belittling(to make sb or the things that sb does seem unimportant) manner.

The Queen’s crown has been kidnapped, Sarge!

Be taken against their will.

Bob, who appears to be a bald, jaundiced child, has pulled the famed sword right from its stone which legend dicatates, makes him the new king.

Bald means people without hair on the head.
Jaundiced means Minions has yellow skin here.(Mostly a diease)
Legend dicatates refers to what a well-known story or myth prescribes or sets as a standard.

Tiny yellow traitor!

A persion who betrays sb or sth, such as a friend, cause, or principle.

I’d wear a dress so sparkly, it glowed!

It glowed conveys a wish or a fantasy about wearing a dress that is exceptionally shiny or radiant.

And you pinheads screwed it up!

Screw it up is a slang expression meaning to make a mistake or mishandle a situation, resulting in negative consequences.

No, no, no, King Bob! You cannot just abdicate the throne!

Abdicate the throne means to relinquish(to stop having sth, especially when this happens unwillingly) or give up one’s position as a monarch or ruler.

If I wasn’t so polite, I’d say this spells certain doom for the country.

Doom refers to an impending(going to happen very soon) negative or disastrous outcome.

I’m a dungeon master. Prepare for torture which I do.

Dungeon is a dark underground room used as a prison, especially in a castle.
Torture means the act of causing sb severe pain in order to punish them or make them say or do sth.

Oh, I’ve got a groovy idea!

Fashionable, attractive and interesting.

Mrs. Overkill, the hair in that picture, it’s just two wavy lines!

Having curves, not straight.

Oh, so, what, now you’re an art critic?

A person who expresses opinions about the good and bad qualities of books, music, etc.

The high collar and cinched waist reflect a simpler, more violent time.

High collar is a fashion detail, prevalent in different periods of history, often stands for formality, modesty, or status.
Cinched waist refers to a waist that has been made smaller or more slender by compression, often using corset or belts. Throughout history, a cinched or tighty-laced waist was beauty standard in various cultures, sybolizing femininity.

The material is a blend of taffeta and high-density body armor.

This is a crisp, smooth, plain woven fabric made from silk or synthetic fibers. It’s known for its shiny surface and is often used in evening dresses.

Must have tiny waist.

To have a slender or narrow waist.

Thanks for doing this, padre.
Come here, let me squeeze you! Oh, you are so squishy!

A person who is qualified to perform religious duties and ceremonies.
Squishy means soft and wet.

Villains, this is no longer a coronation! It is an execution!

Coronation refers to a ceremony where a monarch is formally crowned and inaugurated(the act of formally introducing or initiating sth, particularly with a ceremony ).
Execution refers to the act of killing sb, especially as a legal punishment.

And just for the record, my little deviled eggs, you can thank Kevin for what I’m about to do to you!

Deviled eggs traditionally refers to a dish made from hard-boiled eggs.

You imbecile! Have fun exploding!

It’s an offensive word used to insult someone’s intelligence or decision-making, implying they are stupid or foolish.

The countty owes you a great debt of gratitude.

Gratitude means a feeling of thankfulness or appreciation. Essentially, the statement is a formal way of saying “Thank you for what you’ve done for the country.”

Bob, you were a wise and noble king for all of eight hours.

Noble describes sb with high moral principles, good character, and honor.

Oh，spectacular!

Very impressive.

For you, I have this beautiful, super-duper, incredible snow globe!

This is an informal term used to emphasize the greatness or impressiveness of sth. It’s a way of saying “really great” or “exceptional”.

For your bravery and valor, I am knighting you.

Valor refers to great courage in the face of danger, especially in battle.

Things look bleak, baby, I’m not gonna lie!

Bleak describes a situation that is not hopeful or encouraging, one that is gloomy(without much hope of success or happiness in the future), desolate(very lonely and unhappy), or dreary(makes you feel sad; dull and not interesting).

Are you really going to allow that little penguin to make off with my crown?

Make off means to leave quickly, especially to escape or to steal sth.

He was cunning, he was evil, he was perfect.

Cunning describes sb who is skilled in achieving their goals by deceit(dishonest behaviour that is intended to make sb believe sth that is not true) or evasion(the act of avoiding sb of avoiding sth that you are supposed to do). It suggests cleverness, especially in a sly(acting or done in a secret or dishonest way, often intending to trick people) or manipulative(Skilful at influencing sb or forcing sb to do what you want, often in an unfair way) way.

Angry Bird

I have no choice but to impose the maximum penalty allowed by the law.

Penalty refers to a punishment or consequence imposed for wrongdoing, violation, or offense. This punishment is typically given as a result of breaking rules or laws.

And I’m just super psyched to be taking this journey with you! You’re gonna have a blast.

Psyched is slang that means being very excited, enthusiastic, or eager about something.
Blast is slang for a great and enjoyable time or experience.

You have shared with us the wonders of your quaint, simple little island.

Quaint is adjective that describes sth as having an old-fashioned charm or being attractively unusual or peculiar(strange or unusual, especially in a way that is unpleasant or worrying) in design or appearance.

Say hello to the slingshot!

Slingshot refers a small handheld weapon that consists of a Y-shaped frame with an elastic band attached to the two top prongs.

The trampoline!

A piece of equipment that is used in gymnastics for doing jumps in the air.

I am famished… -it’s chow time!

Famished is an adjective that means extremely hungry.

And now we’ve come to the prestigious Palms district.

Prestigious is used to describe something that is admired or respected, often due to its high status, reputation, or importance.

My friend from the banquet.

A formal meal for a large number of people, usually for a special occasion.

Water is the softest thing, yet it can penetrate mountains and earth.

Penetrate means to pass into or through, often by overcoming resistance.

What the caterpillar calls the end, the world calls a butterfly.

It suggests that what may seem like an ending or a loss can actually be the start of something beautiful and new when viewed from a different perspective.

That’s very lifelike.

Lifelike describes sth that closely resembles or imitates real life.

How do we know you’re not fugitives of the law?

Fugitives refers to persons who are fleeing or escaping from custody (being held by police or other authorities, especially while awaiting trial), typically to avoid arrest or imprisonment. A fugitive can be someone who has committed a crime, is suspected of a crime, or has benn convicted and is avoiding serving their sentence.

You might want to shield your eyes from the sparkle of those trophies.

Trophies refers to prizes or awards given for a particular achievement.

Looking through binoculars, spying on old ladies.

Binoculars refers to an optical instrument with a lens for each eye, used for magnifying and viewing distant objects.

Thanks for your hospitality.

Hospitality refers to the friendly and generous reception and entertainment of guests, visitors, or strangers.

But how do we get from here to there? I am not aquatic in the least.

When referring to animals or plants, “aquatic” means living or growing in water. In this context, when a person uses the term “aquatic,” they are metaphorically suggesting a natural ability or comfort in water, typically in activities like swimming.

Comeon, we’re birds. We’re descended from dinosaurs.

Descended means to have originated from or be related to a particluar ancestor or past form.

And I don’t need any clam, detached, happy birds.

Detached describes someone who is not emotionally involved or connected. A detached individual can seem indifferent, aloof, or distant.

I told you not to mess with me!

To comfront or challenge sb, oftern aggressively.(to tease or play a joke on sb in latter plot)

The treacherous birds have repaid our friendship with an unprovoked act of aggression.

Treacherous is used to describe someone or sth that is guilty of betrayal or deception(the act of deceiving or misleading someone). It implies untrustworthiness or unreliability.
Unprovoked describes an action that is done without any apparent reason or justification. It indicates that the act of aggression was initiated without any prior provocation (the act of doing or saying sth deliberately in order to make sb angry or upset) or reason from the other party.

You’re wrecking my house…You wrecked my houes…Well, now we’re even.

Wreck means to destroy or cause significant damage to sth.
We’re even is an idiomatic expression that means both parties are now equal in terms of debts or obligations (a duty or responsibility to undertake an action or a commitment), often after one person has retaliated or made up for a wrong done to them.

I’m not blushing. I’m just red.

Blushing refers to the involuntary reddening (the automatic or unintentional change in skin color to a reddish hue) of the face, usually triggered by emotions such as embarrassment, shame, or shyness.

You know, back when I was angry, that would have really ticked me off.

Ticked me off is an idiomatic expression that means made me angry or annoyed me.

How did you guys find the time to pull this off? This is why we went to go get pedicures, isn’t it?
Nope. We got pedicures because we’re worth it.

Pull this off is an idiomatic expression that means to successfully achieve or complete something, especially when faced with difficulties or challenges.
A pedicure is a cosmetic (pertains to enhancing or improving appearance) treatment for the feet and toenails.

Well, this just got awkward.
That’ really insensitive.

Insensitive is used to describe sb or sth showing a lack of consideration for another’s feelings, needs, or particular situations. It indicates a lack of empathy or awareness.

Want to go get a bite?
Forget it. Let’s just go home.

Get a bite is an informal way of saying have something to eat or go out to eat.

Roommates! I can’t wait to make a chores list.

The term chores list refers to a list of household tasks or duties that need to be completed.

He’s hatching a new plan.

Originally, hatching refers to the process by which an animal breaks out of an egg. In this context, it means that he is devising, forming, or coming up with a new plan or idea metaphorically.

A Million Miles Away

A hostile rocket… mid-flight?

Hostile describe sth that is unfriendly or adversarial (involving people who are in opposition and who make attacks on each other) in nature.

Having a clear mind during an emergency is imperative.

It means vital importance or crucial, the statement emphasizes that it’s essential to maintain a clear and focused mindset during emergency situations.

I’m busting my ass. My body hurts. I can’t sleep.

The phrase is an informal and coarse (rude and offensive) expression that means working very hard or putting in a lot of effort, often to the physical or mental exhaustion.

It’s as though the whole place is sacred.

Sacred describes sth that is regarded with great respect and reverence (a feeling of great respect or admiration for sb/sth), expecially in a spiritual or religuous context.

I, too, had to work so hard. Tenacity is a superpower.

Tenacity refers to the quality of being very determined, persistent, or unyielding, even in the face of challenges or adversity (a difficult or unpleasant situation).

The safety divers, they’re only there as a precaution.

Precaution refers to a measure taken in advance to prevent something dangerous or undesirable from happening.

Any assistance from the divers will disqualify you.

Disqualify means to declare someone ineligible (not having the necessary qualifications to have or to do sth) or unfit to participate in a particular activity or to be considered for a particular honor due to a specific reason or action.

Flight director LeRoy Cain is now instructing controllers to get out their contingency procedures.

Contingency refers to a future event or circumstance that is possible but cannot be predicted with certainty or one that could cause problems.

A piece of foam insulation broke off the external tank and struck the left wing of the orbiter.

Foam insulation refers to a material used to insulate and protect various structures, particularly in aerospace contexts.

Folks down at the Selection Office, would like to have a word.

Folks down is an informal way to refer to the people or group of individuals at a particular location.
